Indentifying and defining

I have aimed to make this Website for teachers and Puplils of our school and for the parents who are interested in enrolling their in the school. This interactive Website will let students to take a view of the school in a virtual and interactive manner and guide them through their time in this school with the Three dimensinal Map. It will let parents who are interested in enrolling their child/children in the school know more about the school. To create a Three dimensional map of the school, I have used a website called MappedIn which helped me to align the map of the school on the actual location of it. To show the virtual tour of our school, I used a software called Insta 360 and website called Momento360 in order to embed the tour into the website. In order to get feedback from the user, I have created a questions page in google form and embedded it into the website. In terms of safety, the tools I have used are certified safe and valid. All the tools use safety measures in order to protect user data under their own principles. Researching and Planning

I Initially planned my website to be a Static Website i.e, it would be only a informative website for the audience. But going forward I chnaged it into a Dynamic website where people would be able to interact with it. To reach the audience one should come up with a digital marketing technique. These may include social media marketing, email marketing, content marketing, Search engine optimization(SEO) and many more. These techniques will influence users in terms of shaping their opinions and preferences and making them think about it (Products). In this project I have used hybrid project management technique. The reason to choose this technique was that my project was well defined, but thoughout the project I had to make some changes. For example, in the virtual tour section was initially a page embedded from the school website which showed the virtual tour of the school, but the website was too old and didn't showed the present version of the school. So I recorded the 360 degree view of different sections in the school and displayed it in my website.
